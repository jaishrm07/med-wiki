## Practical sequence

The most useful order for learners is:

- describe the wound properly
- decide how sick the patient is
- decide whether source control is needed
- then think antibiotics and follow-up

## Why this is a strong wiki page

This is exactly the kind of page both students and junior doctors will actually use on a ward.
