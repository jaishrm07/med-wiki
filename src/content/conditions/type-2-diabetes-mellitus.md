## Clinical frame

This condition should be understood as more than high glucose. It is a long-term risk-management problem involving:

- glycemia
- kidneys
- eyes
- nerves
- cardiovascular disease

## Why it fits this project

Type 2 diabetes shows how a biochemistry foundation can later evolve into a chronic-disease management page without changing the overall knowledge architecture.
