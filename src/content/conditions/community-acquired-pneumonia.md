## What matters on a first pass

Students and early clinicians should avoid treating pneumonia as only an antibiotic-choice problem. The actual sequence is:

- recognize severity
- identify who needs admission
- start appropriate treatment
- watch carefully for deterioration

## Why this belongs in the wiki

Pneumonia is a good example of a condition page that links anatomy, inflammation, infection, and real clinical decision-making.
