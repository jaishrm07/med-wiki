  - Severe symptomatic anemia with breathlessness, tachycardia, syncope, or cardiac strain

workup:
  - Screen hemoglobin routinely in antenatal care
  - Review diet, prior anemia history, bleeding, and gestational timing
  - Evaluate severity and likely cause before assuming iron deficiency alone
  - Reassess response to supplementation on follow-up

  - Start or optimize iron and folate support based on severity and local practice
  - Address nutrition, adherence, and side-effect barriers early
  - Monitor maternal symptoms and repeat blood counts appropriately

## First-pass frame

The early goal is to answer:

- how severe is the anemia?
- what is the likely cause?
- is routine antenatal management enough or is escalation needed?
