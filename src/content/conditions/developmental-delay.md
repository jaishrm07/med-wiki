redFlags:
  - Loss of previously attained milestones
  - Global delay, seizures, abnormal tone, or concerning neurologic findings
  - Significant social regression, feeding concern, or failure to thrive
workup:
  - Clarify which developmental domains are delayed and by how much
  - Review birth history, hearing and vision, growth, and family concerns
  - Screen for neurologic, nutritional, and psychosocial contributors
  - Decide whether early specialist referral is already indicated
firstLine:
  - Do not rely on reassurance alone when delay is persistent or progressive
  - Use structured developmental screening and counseling
  - Refer early when significant delay or regression is present

  - Regression or multisystem concern
  - Significant delay across multiple domains

## First-pass frame

Students should first decide:

- is this isolated or global?
- is there regression?
- does the child need urgent developmental evaluation?
