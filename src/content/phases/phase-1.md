## What Phase I should feel like

Students usually experience first year as information overload. This phase should instead feel like building a grammar for medicine:

- anatomy gives structure
- physiology gives mechanism
- biochemistry gives molecular logic

## How the site should help

Use the site to move in three directions:

1. year-wise when you need to follow the MBBS sequence
2. subject-wise when you are preparing for internal or prof exams
3. system-wise when you need to integrate concepts and stop memorizing in fragments

## What matters most here

The first build should make difficult first-year topics easier to visualize, easier to revise, and easier to connect to later clinical relevance.
