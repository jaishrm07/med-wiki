## What changes in Phase II

The student mindset shifts here:

- from normal to abnormal
- from pathways to disease mechanisms
- from memorizing drugs to thinking about rational prescribing

## How the site should help

This phase needs shorter, sharper pages with strong tables:

- pathology patterns
- microbiology differentiation points
- drug classes, mechanisms, uses, and adverse effects

## Product note

This is also where the site starts moving closer to eventual doctor-facing utility, because students begin thinking in terms of disease, therapy, and patient decisions.
