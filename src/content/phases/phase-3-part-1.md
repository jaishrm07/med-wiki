## Why this phase matters

This phase often gets mentally separated from the rest of medicine, which is a mistake.

Community medicine is where students begin to understand:

- how disease patterns look at population scale
- how screening and prevention change outcomes
- how health programs and epidemiology connect to practice

## How the site should help

The content here should stay practical:

- field methods
- screening logic
- epidemiology calculations
- public-health program frameworks
