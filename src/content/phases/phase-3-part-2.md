## What the site should become here

By this phase the website should start feeling less like study notes and more like a compact medical reference:

- symptoms and syndromes
- workup steps
- common management pathways
- red flags
- follow-up logic

## Long-term product implication

If you eventually want a doctor-facing wiki, Phase III Part II is the layer where the student product starts blending into clinician workflow.
