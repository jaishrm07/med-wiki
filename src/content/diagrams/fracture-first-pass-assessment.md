## Study move

Run the algorithm mentally on every fracture vignette. If a step feels optional, it usually means that step is the one that will be forgotten on the ward.

## Clinical link

The sequence generalizes beyond one bone. It is the foundation for trauma calls, casualty assessment, and orthopaedic posting discussions.
