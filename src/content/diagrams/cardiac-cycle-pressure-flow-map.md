## Study move

Cover the bottom notes and say the sequence aloud. If you cannot explain why a valve is open or closed, go back to pressure logic instead of memorizing the phase label alone.

## Clinical link

The normal timing map is the baseline that makes valvular lesions, murmurs, and extra heart sounds interpretable later.
