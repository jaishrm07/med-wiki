## Study move

Use the diagram in two passes. First learn the gross shape of the pathway. Then return and memorize only the irreversible checkpoints, ATP accounting, and clinical correlations.

## Clinical link

Anaerobic metabolism, tissue hypoxia, exercise physiology, and some inherited metabolic disorders become easier once this core map is stable.
