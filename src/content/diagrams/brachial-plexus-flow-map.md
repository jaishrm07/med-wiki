## How to use the diagram

Trace the scaffold from left to right first. Only after the main ladder feels automatic should you start attaching named branches or lesion patterns.

## Common study mistake

Students often jump straight to branch memorization and skip the structural logic. That makes the diagram look crowded when the real problem is sequencing.
