## Study move

Practice the sequence out loud on normal films before trying to diagnose disease. A stable normal reading pattern is what makes abnormal films readable later.

## Clinical link

This framework supports pneumonia review, pulmonary edema screening, tube-checking, and first-pass interpretation during ward calls.
