## Best mental model

Think of glycolysis as the shortest path from glucose to quick usable energy.

## What to memorize selectively

- the irreversible checkpoints
- phosphofructokinase as the key control point
- net ATP and NADH production

## What to understand deeply

- why anaerobic glycolysis matters
- why regulation changes with cellular energy status
