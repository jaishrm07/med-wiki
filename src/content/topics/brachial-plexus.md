## Big picture

The plexus is easier if students stop treating it as a giant list and start seeing it as a flowchart.

## How to study it

- memorize the main ladder first
- then map terminal branches
- then attach lesion patterns to specific levels

## What students usually mix up

- roots versus trunks
- cords versus branches
- upper trunk versus lower trunk lesions
