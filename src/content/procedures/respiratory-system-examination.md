  - Start with inspection for respiratory distress, symmetry, effort, and accessory muscle use

  - Percuss both sides in a planned sequence rather than randomly
  - Auscultate systematically and relate added sounds to the likely disease process

## Practical frame

Students should aim to say what they found, what pattern it suggests, and how sick the patient appears right now.

## Why this page exists

Respiratory examination is a bridge skill. It connects anatomy, physiology, imaging, and acute bedside judgment.
