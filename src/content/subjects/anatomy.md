## What good anatomy content looks like

Students need anatomy explained in layers:

- the clean conceptual scaffold
- the memorable diagram
- the clinical implication

## What to avoid

Avoid building anatomy pages as giant unlabeled dumps of facts. Every page should answer:

- what is the organizing structure?
- what are the relationships worth remembering?
- what lesions or procedures make this useful later?
